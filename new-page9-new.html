How it works
Our 4 piece #FOBO® kit is your complete, breakthrough acne solution that treats acne from the gut level as well as on the skin surface by using YOUR microbiome. Our complete kit has everything you need for clear, healthy, beautiful skin.
  • SCANTILY Pad® AM/PM Acne Treatment works to treat and control acne breakouts by rebalancing your skin’s microbiome, killing acne causing bacteria and allowing healthy bacteria to flourish. The unique combination of Patented SE Microbiome Complex® and Salicylic Acid (BHA) work together to treat current acne breakouts, clear pores, and prevent future acne breakouts, while Aloe Vera soothes and hydrates the skin. SCANTILY Pad® delivers long-lasting effects without irritation. As a result, pimples heal faster with less inflammation, redness, pain, hyper-pigmentation (dark spots), and scarring - leaving clear, healthy, beautiful skin.
  • CLEANSE with Benefits™ AM/PM Cleanser removes oil and dirt from the skin with soap and sulphate free gentle cleansers, and hydrates and protects the skin with glycerin and Panthenol pro-vitamin B5. Patented SE Microbiome Complex® (our proprietary combination of naturally occurring prebiotics and postbiotics) supports a healthy microbiome by killing acne causing bacteria while promoting the growth of acne-fighting bacteria - the result is clean, balanced, acne-free skin.
  • SUPPLEMEANT to Be® Daily Pre + Probiotic Mix is the perfect daily supplement for acne sufferers. Our unique blend of scientifically proven ingredients were specifically selected for their acne-fighting and skin health benefits - working both in the gut and at skin level. Prebiotics provide the food source for good, acne-fighting bacteria and 6.25 billion CFU of probiotics increase the numbers of acne-fighting bacteria to kill acne-causing bacteria. Zinc helps to decrease oil production in the skin and helps to reduce inflammation; Vitamins A, C and E work together to provide super-antioxidant skin protection and spinach extract contains enzymes and antioxidants for optimal skin health. Safe for children and adults.
  • PATCH Me (If You Can)® our drug-free, clear, adhesive pimple patches are made from hydrocolloid which works by drawing out and absorbing pus and oil from pimples to reduce pimple redness, oiliness and overall acne severity. These moisture rich patches provide a physical barrier to prevent picking and spread of bacteria. They also lower the pH in the area under the patch which inhibits bacterial growth and protects against UVB to help prevent hyperpigmentation (brown spots) and scarring.
how to use
  • CLEANSE With Benefits™: Cleanse the skin and pat dry. Use one pad every morning and every evening to gently apply the solution all over the face, avoiding the eye area. We recommend starting with one application per day (either morning or evening) to ensure excess skin drying doesn’t occur, then gradually increase to morning and evening application. If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs, reduce application to once a day or every other day until your skin has adjusted.
  • SCANTILY Pad® Cleanse the skin with CLEANSE With Benefits™ and pat dry. Use one pad every morning and every evening to gently apply the solution all over the face, avoiding the eye area. We recommend starting with one application per day (either morning or evening) to ensure excess skin drying doesn’t occur, then gradually increase to morning and evening application. If bothersome dryness or peeling occurs, reduce application to once a day or every other day until your skin has adjusted.
  • PATCH Me (If You Can)® use after applying SCANTILY Pad®. Peel a patch from the sheet and apply it onto a pimple. Leave it on for at least an hour or overnight for best results. A change in color to white indicates pus absorbance. Remove the patch by peeling it off. If necessary, use warm water for easier removal.
  • SUPPLEMEANT To Be® Take one capsule daily before or with a meal. Either swallow the capsule whole with water; or twist the capsule, pull apart and sprinkle the contents on food or in a drink of your choice.
